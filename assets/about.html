Catching the bus has never been more legen... wait for it... DARY! With "Wait For It..." for Android, you get a snappy interface to get real-time predictions of the busses, trains, and cable cars in San Francisco.

Wait For It... uses NEXTbus web services to find up-to-the-minute wait times.
